## v1.0.0 — 2026-03-31

Initial release of all four IIS Typst templates on [Typst Universe](https://typst.app/universe).

### Added

- **`ethz-iis-dissertation`** — PhD dissertation template following ETH Zurich doctoral
  regulations, with official and Hartung-Gorre series render modes, running chapter
  headers, chip gallery appendix, and CV page.
- **`ethz-iis-thesis`** — Thesis/semester project report template for Master, Bachelor,
  and semester projects at IIS.
- **`ethz-iis-research-plan`** — PhD research plan template.
- **`ethz-iis-assignment`** — Assignment sheet template for thesis supervisors.
